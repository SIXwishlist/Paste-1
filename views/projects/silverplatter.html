Silver Platter

CSS/PHP/SQL Development

Silver Platter is the best source to find live music in Phoenix. It started as a simple mailing list full of shows and quickly became "Your Weekly Dish of Desert Culture." I designed the original site and built a custom event system which utilizes a database of artists and venues to fill out the calendar.

Note: The current site is no longer under my control, and while it retains some interface elements, should not be considered my work.
